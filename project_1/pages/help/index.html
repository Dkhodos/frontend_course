Help & Support

Welcome to the OnoAir Help & Support page. Here you'll find information on how to use the application and get the most out of your experience.

How to Book a Flight

To book a flight, navigate to the "Book a Flight" section. Use the filters to select your origin and destination, then choose a flight from the available options and enter passenger details.

Managing Bookings

View all your upcoming and past bookings in the "My Bookings" section. Here, you can review details for each booking and manage your reservations.

Admin Management

Administrators have access to additional management features, including adding new flights and destinations. Use the "Manage Flights" and "Manage Destinations" sections to update the database.

Need Further Assistance?

If you need more help, please contact our support team or refer to the detailed user guide provided in this section.
