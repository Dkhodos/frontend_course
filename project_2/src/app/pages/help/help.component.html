Welcome to the OnoAir Help & Support page. Find all the information you need to use the application and enhance your experience.

How to Book a Flight

Navigate to the "Book a Flight" section. Use filters to select your origin and destination, choose from available flights, and enter passenger details.

Managing Bookings

Access "My Bookings" to review your upcoming and past bookings. You can manage your reservations and check flight details.

Admin Management

Admins can manage flights and destinations via the "Manage Flights" and "Manage Destinations" sections.

Need Further Assistance?

Contact our support team or refer to the detailed user guide for more assistance.
